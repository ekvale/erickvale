About the Rights Archive

This archive collects and indexes articles related to human rights violations, constitutional violations, police misconduct, government overreach, civil liberties, and similar topics.

  • Sources: RSS/Atom feeds (e.g. ACLU, Human Rights Watch, investigative outlets). Add feeds in Django admin and run fetch_rights_feeds on a schedule.
  • Add by URL: Manually add a single article by URL; title/summary are fetched when possible.
  • Tags: Classify articles (human rights, constitutional, civil liberties, etc.). Manage tags in admin.
  • Search: Full-text search over title, summary, and content. Filter by tag, source, and date.
  • Export: Export filtered results to CSV for reports and briefs.

Access is staff-only. Respect copyright and terms of use when storing content.
