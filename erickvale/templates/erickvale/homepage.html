Where we focus

HTAC & federated analytics

Token-based linkage, deduplicated rosters, and suppressed prevalence reporting so collaboratives can publish community-level insight without a “collect it all” warehouse.

Public-health data modernization

Cloud-ready pipelines, open standards (OMOP / OHDSI), and quality routines that match where philanthropy and agencies are investing: fewer brittle silos, more reproducible evidence.

Agentic AI in care & governance

Workflows that pair LLMs with clear human accountability—documentation assistance, grant intelligence, and triage support bounded by DUAs, audit logs, and “human-in-the-loop” release gates.

Data governance & interoperability

Stewardship design, consent-aligned sharing, FHIR and CDM handoffs, and cross-sector interoperability patterns that respect FQHCs, Tribal nations, and justice-impacted data as first-class governance problems.
